I created an abstract painting tool for the user to play with. I made various brushes for the users to use and explore with.
1 = simple blue line, 2 = triangle with a blue border,3 = parallel brush with random colors assigned to each stroke, 4 = triangle brush but is confined to the center,5 = circle brush, 6 = brown line, 7 = crazy brush,8 = sticker, 9 = eraser tool
X = clears the the canvas / makes it white, T = makes the background black, P = saves your artwork. Have Fun!!
