Using Twine, this was my first attempt at creating a choice based game. I created this as my final for Art 74. I was aiming for a 2000s flash game look, where it was super simple and didn't have much to it other than a picture background and the character. The game is about helping Beatrix navigate through her day, but while playing the Player begins to notice some weird things happening.
