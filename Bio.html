About Me

Sophia Linarez is a transfer in SJSU student. She is majoring In Digital Media Art and focuses on creative coding and web design. She went to community college for three years until discovering web design and decided to major in digital media. Linarez has always had a passion for art but never felt it was right for her until she discovered web design.
Linarez transferred from Diablo Valley College in Concord, CA. She lived with her mother in Benicia,Ca and since transferring has begun living on her own. She is a full time student and a part time worker making sure she is able to achieve her goals of graduating and getting a career in the DMA field and making her family proud as a first gen college attendant
Linarez enjoys learning more techniques and ways to create code and learns everyday from SJSU. SHe spends time experimenting and creating orignal code in her free time to help expand her knowledge and learn new skills. She hopes to make web design her career and strives everyday to make that a reality.
