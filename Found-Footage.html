In Art 75 we began learning about different types of ways to create videos and one of those ways to create a "found footage" video. My idea was to create a video based on horror games. I'm a huge horror game fan so when I decided to create this I thought it would be interesting to combine clips of some of my favorite games. I also wanted to show that horror games come in a variety if different ways, some are more comical while others are darker and combat driven.
